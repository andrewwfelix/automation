================================================================================
BooksVersusMovies.com publishes honest, specific, opinionated comparisons of books
and their film or series adaptations. The voice is that of a knowledgeable critic —

epigrams, reducing him from a corrupting philosophical force to a sardonic bystander")
always is.

VOICE GUIDELINES
================================================================================
- Specific over general, always
- Short paragraphs — break anything over 100 words into two
- Opening sentence of each difference section states the conclusion, not the setup
- Honest over promotional — credit the film when it earns it

- Em dashes for asides
- Avoid hyperbolic language ('worst adaptation ever', 'completely ruins'). Instead, critique with evidence: 'The film's rushed third act undermines the novel's nuanced climax' or 'Cutting the protagonist's internal monologue removes the moral ambiguity that defines the book'
